Welcome!

Este app foi desenhado para te levar do zero ao inglês falado de forma simples, natural e altamente eficaz. Cada sessão dura aproximadamente 10 minutos.

🗺️

What's Inside

  • 🏛️ Temples: Track your progress across English-speaking cities.
  • 🃏 Flashcards: Memorise words with the FSRS-4.5 algorithm.
  • 🎯 Quiz: Test your knowledge and earn XP.
  • 📖 Vocabulary: Translation, IPA pronunciation and examples.
  • 📚 Grammar: Lessons from A1 to B2.
  • 📜 Reading: Read authentic texts in immersion mode.
  • 💬 Dialogues: Practise real everyday conversations.
  • 🎵 Songs: Learn with English songs.
  • 🎤 Listen & Repeat: Train your pronunciation.
🚀

Where to Start?

É muito fácil começar sua jornada:

  1. Go to the TEMPLES tab
  2. Choose the 1st Temple (New York — The Foundations)
  3. Study using the FLASHCARDS
  4. Test yourself with the QUIZZES!

💡 Tap ❓ at the top at any time to replay the interactive app tour!
